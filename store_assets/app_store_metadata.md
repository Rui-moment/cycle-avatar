## App Store Description

### English

**Title**: CycleAvatar - Smart Fitness Tracker

**Subtitle**: Recovery-based training with avatar growth

**Description**:
Transform your fitness journey with CycleAvatar, the intelligent workout tracker that makes recovery science fun and accessible. Watch your avatar grow as you train smarter, not just harder.

**Key Features:**
• 🎯 **Smart Recovery Tracking**: Visual muscle group recovery status
• 🚀 **Quick Workout Logging**: Log sets in under 10 seconds
• 🎮 **Avatar Growth System**: Your avatar grows when you train optimally
• 📊 **Intelligent Plan Suggestions**: AI-powered workout recommendations
• 📱 **Offline-First Design**: Works perfectly without internet
• 🏆 **Progress Tracking**: PR celebrations and streak counters
• 🔔 **Smart Notifications**: Recovery-based training reminders
• 🌍 **Multi-language Support**: English and Japanese

**Perfect for:**
- Fitness beginners who want to avoid overtraining
- Experienced lifters seeking optimization
- Anyone who wants sustainable training habits

**Science-Based Approach:**
CycleAvatar uses proven fatigue and recovery models to help you train at the optimal time for each muscle group, maximizing gains while minimizing injury risk.

**Privacy-First:**
Your data stays on your device. Optional cloud sync for backup only.
