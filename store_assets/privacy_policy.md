# CycleAvatar Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: [Date]

## Introduction

CycleAvatar ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address, display name
- **Workout Data**: Exercise logs, weights, repetitions, RPE ratings
- **Preferences**: Language settings, notification preferences
- **Optional Information**: Age range, fitness goals (for personalized recommendations)

### Information Automatically Collected
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Analytics**: App crashes, performance metrics (anonymized)
- **Local Storage**: All workout data is stored locally on your device

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- **Core Functionality**: Provide workout tracking and avatar growth features
- **Personalization**: Generate workout recommendations based on your data
- **Progress Tracking**: Calculate recovery states and training metrics
- **Notifications**: Send recovery-based training reminders

### Secondary Uses
- **App Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
- **Customer Support**: Respond to your inquiries and technical issues
- **Legal Compliance**: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- **Primary Storage**: All workout data is stored locally on your device
- **Encryption**: Local database is encrypted using industry-standard methods
- **No Cloud Requirement**: App functions completely offline

### Optional Cloud Sync
- **User Choice**: Cloud sync is entirely optional
- **Encrypted Transit**: Data is encrypted during transmission
- **Encrypted Storage**: Cloud data is encrypted at rest
- **User Control**: You can disable cloud sync at any time

### Security Measures
- **Data Encryption**: All sensitive data is encrypted
- **Secure Transmission**: HTTPS/TLS for all network communications
- **Access Controls**: Strict access controls for any cloud data
- **Regular Updates**: Security patches and updates

## Data Sharing

### We Do Not Share
- **Personal Workout Data**: Never shared with third parties
- **Personal Information**: Email, name, or personal details
- **Individual Metrics**: Your specific performance data

### Limited Sharing
- **Anonymized Analytics**: Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics
- **Legal Requirements**: Only if required by law or legal process
- **Service Providers**: Encrypted data with trusted service providers (cloud storage only)

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Control
- **Access**: View all your stored data within the app
- **Export**: Download your complete workout history
- **Delete**: Permanently delete your account and all data
- **Modify**: Update your information and preferences

### Communication Preferences
- **Notifications**: Control all notification types and timing
- **Marketing**: We do not send marketing communications
- **Updates**: Receive important app updates and security notices

### Account Management
- **Account Deletion**: Request complete account deletion
- **Data Portability**: Export your data in standard formats
- **Sync Control**: Enable or disable cloud synchronization
